THE NEXT ERA OF
COUNTER‑STRIKE
BEGINS THIS SUMMER
Counter-Strike 2 is the most significant technical advancement in Counter-Strike history and will allow us to continue adding features and updates for many years to come.

We'll be unveiling all of the game's new features this summer when it's officially released, but the road to Counter-Strike 2 begins today with the limited test, available to a selection of CS:GO players. During the test, we will evaluate a subset of CS2's features in order to address potential issues prior to its global release.

Counter-Strike 2 is coming this summer as a free CS:GO update, so choose your gear, hone your skills and get ready for what's to come!

Read on to learn more about some of the features of the Counter-Strike 2 limited trial.
Your items in a new light
Bring your entire CS:GO inventory to Counter-Strike 2. Not only will you keep all the items you've collected over the years, but they'll look even better with Source 2's lighting and materials.

In addition to being compatible with classic models and finishes, all stock weapons have been upgraded with high-resolution models, and some weapon finishes take advantage of these new models.

A TURNING POINT

Smoke grenades are now dynamic volumetric objects that interact with the environment and react to lighting, gunfire and explosions.

Gameplay

Smoke now has the ability to interact with other events in the game, creating new possibilities. Bullets and AE grenades can displace it to briefly clear lines of sight or expand occlusion.

It expands to fill in spaces naturally.

Smoke will now seep through open doors and broken windows, travel down and up stairs, spread through elongated corridors and combine with other smoke.

Reacts to lighting

In Counter-Strike 2, smoke particles work with the unified lighting system, allowing for more realistic light and color.
